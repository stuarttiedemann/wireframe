About Us

Mosquitos? Yellow Flies?

Take back your yard today!

Are you tired of not being able to enjoy your yard in the summer without being bit? South Walton Mosquito Control is working this year to protect honeybees by only spraying the roads after dark, and only in areas where testing has proven that there are high levels of mosquitoes. As a result, the yellow fly population is out of control. Now homeowners can have their own personal mosquito misting system installed that automatically mists a similar chemical around the perimeter of their home and yard. The system sprays at dawn and dusk when mosquitoes are more prevalent. The system can also be activated by included wireless remote or by the smartphone app to kill and repel during daylight (when yellow flies are more active) prior to enjoying your yard.
